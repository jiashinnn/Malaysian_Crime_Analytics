Crime in Malaysia: Overview

Malaysia has seen significant changes in crime patterns over the years. Key insights include:

Declining Trend

Overall crime rates have generally declined over the past decade due to improved policing and prevention strategies.

Urban Concentration

Crime remains concentrated in urban areas, with significant variations between states and districts.

Evolving Types

While traditional crime types show declines, newer forms like cybercrime have been increasing in recent years.
