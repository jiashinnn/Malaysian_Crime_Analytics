Dashboard Information

This interactive dashboard provides comprehensive visualization of crime statistics across Malaysia. The dashboard features:

  • Geographic Distribution: View crime patterns across states and districts
  • Temporal Trends: Analyze how crime rates have changed over time
  • Crime Categories: Break down data by different crime categories and types
  • Comparative Analysis: Compare statistics between different regions and time periods

Use the interactive filters and controls within the dashboard to customize your view and explore specific aspects of the data.
